Rules

Each participant enters his name and the name of the character. All of them are entered in the table. After starting the game, you get a character that your friend wrote down. But you don't know what this character is. You need to guess it by asking questions. You can only ask such questions, the answer to which is Yes or No. If the answer to your question is no, the turn goes to the next player.
Purpose of the game: first of all to guess your character.
